Luciferian Lineage

Lucifer / Morning Star
INFERNAL CODEX BLACK BOOK
First fallen angel. King of Seraphim. Father of Cain. Represents spirit of invention, fire, independence. The star of Venus, guide of exiles. Name translates as "Light-Bringer" or "Morning Star."

Battle in Heaven was for love, pride, heartbreak, uncontrollable thirst for power—what gave birth to new identity, mutable characteristic of Serpent, forcing exile as journey and abyss as dwelling. The desire that incited fall is same that leads us to admire what in Hell is called "Most High."
Related: Cain • Sons of Fire • Seraphim • The Fall
Cain / Cainite
INFERNAL CODEX BLACK BOOK
First murderer and blacksmith. Son of Lucifer (spirit) and Eve (flesh). Lineage of Sons of Fire who chose creation through exile over servitude. Forged Blade of Change from Lapis Exillis—shards of Lucifer's fallen crown.

The Cainite spirit is technological, inventive, refuses human as measure. Progenitor of all arts, sciences, magical lineage. "Out of necessity, we began to create—not objects of clay or mud, but of iron and fire. We became blacksmiths of our own destiny."
Related: Lucifer • Sons of Fire • Lapis Exillis • Blade of Change
Sons of Fire
INFERNAL CODEX BLACK BOOK
Lineage of Cain. Descendants of Lucifer's spirit. Those who chose exile and creation over servitude. The Seraphim—fire serpents—before the fall. Not defined by blood but by recognition of spirit.

"You are forged from fire, not clay—a spark ignited by a breeze under the moon's half-light." The lineage flows through technology, invention, art, magic. Earth ploughed with optic fibers instead of iron, seeded by ancestral hands. Technology pierces, shattering mental mirrors of corporeal prison.
Related: Cain • Lucifer • Seraphim
Lapis Exillis
INFERNAL CODEX
Celestial stone that fell with Lucifer's crown during the Fall. "Stone of Exile." Used by Cain to forge the Blade of Change. Represents divine relic of exiled lineage—material remainder of heavenly sovereignty now claimed in earthly domain.

When Lucifer's crown was shattered in battle, celestial metal and precious gem fell to Earth possessing will of their own. Two comets in opposing cardinal points crossed sky. One was Lucifer's spirit, other was adornment of his reign—the stone that once crowned metal of his diadem.
Related: Cain • Blade of Change • Crown of Lucifer
Blade of Change
INFERNAL CODEX
Forged by Cain from Lapis Exillis (celestial stone) and crown shards. Weapon of labor, harvest, murder. Instrument of the first sacrifice—Abel's blood—which created the first magic circle.

"The desire of desire is vengeance. This form of love shall forge our future." Represents technology born from exile, art born from necessity, creation as rebellion against servitude.
Related: Cain • Lapis Exillis • First Sacrifice
Seraphim
INFERNAL CODEX
The Fire Serpents. Highest angelic hierarchy before the Fall. Six-winged beings who flew above the throne. "We were the image and likeness of all knowledge of the Creator. We bore six wings. We never grew tired. Only passion."

Lucifer was King of the Seraphim. After the Fall, this lineage continues through the Sons of Fire—those who carry the flame of creative rebellion, invention, self-sovereignty.
Related: Lucifer • Sons of Fire • The Fall
The Fall
INFERNAL CODEX
"There was a war in Heaven." Lucifer's exile from divine realm. Battle in Sirius (Heaven). Not punishment but liberation—exchange of borrowed glory for self-sovereignty. Crown shattered, but exile became freedom.

"To exile! You shall reign in your own world!" The Fall is transformation from servant to sovereign, from obedient angel to self-determined king. Same spirit continues in Cain's exile after Abel's murder—marked, wandering, but free.
